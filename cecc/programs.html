"Programs for Young Children"

Children's Montessori, Pre-School and Daycare Programs

Children's Edu-Care centers offer full or part time infant, toddler, and pre-school programs and school age daycare at our child care facilities Monday through Friday, 6:00am to 6:30pm.

We offer the following pre-school and daycare programs led by degreed, experienced teachers:

Mid Rivers Day Care 6:00am - 6:30pm

Cuddle Care
Personal, caring attention infant through two years

Kinder-Prep
Traditional pre-school and PreK three through six years

Children's Edu-Care Center 6:00am - 6:30pm

The Academy
Montessori Primary two and a half through six years

Kindergarten
Preparing for elementary five through six years

Kountry Klub
Before/after school, days off daycare six through twelve years

Kountry Kamp
Summer camp six through twelve years

Breakfast, lunch and afternoon snack follow USDA guidelines and are monitored by the Missouri Department of Health.

You can Schedule a Tour online or call us anytime between 6:00am - 6:30pm. We look forward to hearing from you!
